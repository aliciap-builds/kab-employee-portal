Section 1

Hours & Pay

M–F Business Days
8–5 Shop Hours
40 hrs Standard Workweek
Bi-weekly Pay Schedule

Timekeeping

  • Clock in and out every shift — missed punches must be reported to HR
  • Timecards must be verified for accuracy by Tuesday before payday
  • All time worked (including device use for work) must be logged
  • Never clock in for another employee — falsifying records may result in termination

Overtime

  • All overtime must be pre-approved by your supervisor
  • 1.5× pay for hours over 8/day or 40/week
  • 2× pay (double time) for hours over 12 in a day
  • Special rates apply for the 7th consecutive workday
  • Unauthorized overtime is subject to disciplinary action

Meal & Break Periods

  • 15-min paid breaks required every 4 hours (your responsibility to take)
  • 30-min unpaid lunch required if you work 5+ hours
  • If working 6+ hours, lunch must be taken by the 5th hour
  • Suggested lunch window: 11:30 AM – 1:00 PM
  • Second 30-min meal break required for shifts over 10 hours

Paydays & Deductions

  • Paid bi-weekly; if payday falls on a holiday, you're paid the day before
  • Paychecks not released to anyone but the employee (without written authorization)
  • Direct deposit available — you'll receive an itemized wage statement
  • Questions about deductions? Contact HR/Payroll immediately
Attendance Policy: Contact your supervisor directly before your shift if you'll be absent. Voicemail/text is only acceptable in extreme emergencies, and a follow-up call must be made that day. No-call for 3 consecutive days is treated as voluntary resignation.

Accrual rate: 1 PTO hour earned per 27.40 hours worked. Full-time employees also receive 16 hours up front at the start of each year.

PTO Rules at a Glance

  • Full day off: Request at least 1 week in advance
  • Partial day: 48-hour notice required
  • Sick/emergency: Notify supervisor as early as possible; call again each additional day
  • Cash out: Up to 40 hrs cashed out on last paycheck of the year only
  • Advance PTO: Up to 16 hrs can be borrowed from next quarter with manager approval
  • PTO can only be used to fill missed hours in a regular 40-hour week

Benefits

Eligible after first 60 days of employment (coverage starts the 1st of the following month).

Retirement Plan

Full-time employees are eligible to participate in the company-sponsored retirement plan after 365 days of employment.

Review the Summary Plan Description (SPD) for full details. Contact HR/Payroll for plan documents.

Workers' Compensation

If you're injured on the job, you are covered — at no cost to you. Covers medical treatment and a portion of lost wages.

Section 4

Conduct & Workplace Rules

Zero Tolerance: The following result in immediate termination — no progressive steps: workplace violence, harassment, theft of any kind, working for a competitor, vandalism, unauthorized use of company vehicles/equipment, and divulging confidential company information.

Standards We Hold

  • Act and dress professionally at all times
  • Always be courteous and respectful to customers and partners
  • Keep your work area clean and organized
  • Respect coworkers' space, tools, and property
  • Take pride in your work and own your mistakes
  • Personal calls taken on breaks, outside the production area
  • Follow KAB Standard Operating Procedures

Disciplinary Process

  1. Warning #1 — Documented; sit-down with plan of resolution
  2. Warning #2 — Documented; revised plan. Employee signs acknowledgment that next incident leads to suspension
  3. Suspension & Probation — Suspension without pay; 30-day probation period after return
  4. Termination — All belongings removed and company property returned within 48 hrs

Dress Code

Production team — provided 10 shirts + 5 pants. Keep uniforms clean; report issues immediately.

Office team — business casual (polos, button-downs, slacks, dark jeans, closed-toe shoes). Bottoms past knee; tops covering shoulders and back.

Not allowed: light-wash jeans, torn jeans, t-shirts, hoodies, open-toe shoes, leggings/yoga pants.

Tools & Equipment

  • You are responsible for tools assigned to you
  • Lost or damaged tools due to negligence = deducted from your paycheck
  • Shared tools: cost split among responsible parties (or whole team if no party identified)
  • Normal wear and tear is covered by KAB
  • No tools or supplies leave the shop without manager permission

Drugs, Alcohol & Smoking

  • Zero tolerance for alcohol or illegal drugs on company property or while on company business
  • Prescription medications OK — notify supervisor if they may impair your performance
  • Smoke-free workplace: no smoking, vaping, or e-cigarettes anywhere on premises
  • Random drug/alcohol testing may be required

Harassment & Reporting

Harassment of any kind — sexual, racial, or otherwise — is strictly prohibited and grounds for termination.

How to report: Tell your supervisor, or contact HR directly. You can also bypass your supervisor and go directly to James Kim or Alicia Pham.

Retaliation against anyone who reports harassment is also prohibited. CA employees may also file with DFEH at (800) 884-1684.

Loyalty Policy: Employees may not work for any competitor of KAB. Violation results in immediate termination.

Training & Career Development

$500 Annual Training Credit
6 mo. Complete Initial Training
$500 I-CAR Pro Level 2 Bonus

I-CAR Requirements

  • Year 1: Obtain and maintain I-CAR Pro Level 1 certification
  • Year 2: Obtain and maintain I-CAR Pro Level 2 certification
  • Completing Pro Level 2 earns a $500 bonus
  • Failing to meet these milestones may result in probation, suspension, or termination

Training Policy

  • All courses must be approved by your supervisor in advance
  • KAB provides a $500/year credit toward approved training
  • Courses not successfully completed must be retaken at your own expense
  • Any training balance owed is due upon termination or resignation

Team Meetings

  • Monthly Production Meetings: 1st Friday of every month — mandatory for production team
  • Quarterly All-Hands: Month after quarter-end, after hours, dinner provided — optional and unpaid
  • Changes from quarterly meetings are posted in the break room or available on request
