Hours & Pay

Weekly Pay Schedule

🕐 Timekeeping

  • Clock in and out every shift — report missed punches to HR immediately
  • Verify your timecard accuracy by Monday before payday
  • All work time must be logged, including device use for work tasks
  • Never clock in for another employee — falsifying records may result in termination

⏱ Overtime Rules

  • All overtime must be pre-approved by your supervisor
  • 1.5× pay for hours over 8/day or 40/week
  • 2× pay (double time) for hours over 12 in a single day
  • Special rates apply on the 7th consecutive workday
  • Unauthorized overtime is subject to disciplinary action

🍽 Meals & Breaks

  • 15-min paid breaks required every 4 hours (your responsibility to take)
  • 30-min unpaid lunch required if you work 5+ hours
  • If working 6+ hours, lunch must be taken by the 5th hour
  • Suggested lunch window: 11:30 AM – 1:00 PM
  • Second meal break required for shifts over 10 hours
  • Notice some of these can be disregarded if a meal waiver is on file. If you'd like one, reach out to HR

💵 Paydays

  • Paid weekly
  • If payday falls on a holiday, you'll be paid the day before
  • Paychecks only released to the employee (written authorization required for others)
  • Direct deposit available — you'll receive an itemized wage statement
  • Questions about deductions? Contact HR immediately
Attendance: Contact your supervisor directly before your shift if you'll be absent. Voicemail/text is only acceptable in extreme emergencies, and a follow-up call must be made the same day. No-call for 3 consecutive days is treated as voluntary resignation.

Accrual rate: 1 PTO hour per 27.40 hours worked. Full-time employees also receive 16 hours upfront at the start of each year.

📋 PTO Rules at a Glance

  • Full day off: Request at least 1 week in advance
  • Partial day: 48-hour notice required
  • Sick/emergency: Notify supervisor ASAP; call again each additional day out
  • Cash out: Up to 40 hrs on last paycheck of the year only
  • Advance PTO: Up to 16 hrs from next quarter with manager approval
  • PTO can only be used to fill missed hours in a regular 40-hr week

🛡 Other Protected Leave (CA)

  • Jury Duty: Unpaid; may use PTO. Provide summons to supervisor ASAP

  • Pregnancy Disability: Up to 4 months leave

Benefits

Health · Retirement · Workers' Comp · Referral

🏥 Health Insurance

Eligible after your first 60 days — coverage begins the 1st of the following month.

  • KAB covers 50% of your monthly medical premium
  • KAB covers $25/month toward dental
  • Adding a spouse or dependents: KAB covers 25%, employee pays the rest

Contact HR for plan details and enrollment forms.

💼 Retirement Plan

Full-time employees are eligible to enroll in the company-sponsored retirement plan after 365 days of employment.

Review the Summary Plan Description (SPD) for full details. Contact HR for plan documents.

🦺 Workers' Compensation

If you're injured on the job, you're covered — at no cost to you. Covers medical treatment and a portion of lost wages.

  • Report all injuries to your supervisor immediately
  • Supervisor contacts HR to get claim forms
  • No enrollment required — automatic coverage

👥 Employee Referral Bonus

Refer a qualified candidate who gets hired and completes 90 days of employment and you earn a $500 bonus.

All referred candidates go through the standard hiring process. Direct questions to HR.

Conduct & Workplace Rules

Standards · Discipline · Dress Code · Safety
Zero Tolerance — Immediate Termination: Workplace violence, harassment, theft of any kind, working for a competitor, vandalism, unauthorized use of company vehicles/equipment, and divulging confidential company information.

✅ Standards We Hold

  • Act and dress professionally at all times
  • Always be courteous and respectful to customers and partners
  • Keep your work area clean and organized
  • Respect coworkers' space, tools, and property
  • Take pride in your work and own your mistakes
  • Personal calls taken on breaks, outside the production area
  • Follow KAB Standard Operating Procedures

⚠️ Disciplinary Process

  1. Warning #1 — Documented; sit-down with plan of resolution
  2. Warning #2 — Documented; revised plan. Employee signs acknowledgment that next incident leads to suspension
  3. Suspension & Probation — Unpaid suspension; 30-day probation after return
  4. Termination — All company property returned within 48 hrs; final check issued after return

👔 Dress Code

Production team: Provided 10 shirts + 5 pants. Keep uniforms clean and report issues immediately.

Office team: Business casual — polos, button-downs, slacks, dark jeans, closed-toe shoes. Bottoms past the knee; tops covering shoulders and back.

Not allowed: light-wash jeans, torn jeans, t-shirts, hoodies, open-toe shoes, leggings/yoga pants.

🔧 Tools & Equipment

  • You are responsible for all tools assigned to you
  • Lost or damaged tools due to negligence are deducted from your paycheck
  • Shared tools: cost split among responsible parties (or whole team if no one identified)
  • Normal wear and tear is covered by KAB
  • No tools or supplies may leave the shop without manager permission

🚭 Drugs, Alcohol & Smoking

  • Zero tolerance for alcohol or illegal drugs on company property or while on company business
  • Prescription medications are OK — notify supervisor if they may impair your performance
  • Smoke-free workplace: no smoking, vaping, or e-cigarettes anywhere on premises
  • Random drug/alcohol testing may be required

🚨 Harassment & Reporting

Harassment of any kind — sexual, racial, or otherwise — is strictly prohibited and grounds for termination.

How to report: Tell your supervisor, or contact Alicia (HR) or James Kim directly. Retaliation against anyone who reports is also prohibited.

CA employees may also file with DFEH at (800) 884-1684 or dfeh.ca.gov.

Loyalty Policy: Employees may not work for any competitor of KAB. Violation results in immediate termination.

🎓 I-CAR Requirements

  • Any outstanding training balance is due upon termination or resignation
